Banana Chocolate Chip Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ white flour
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ brown sugar
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • ripe bananas
  • chocolate chips
    • chocolate chips

Description

  1. Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt and sugar well
  2. Add Oil and eggs to flour mixture
  3. Mix well and add Banana and Chocolate Chips
  4. place in oiled loaf tine
  5. Bake at 350 for 60 minutes
